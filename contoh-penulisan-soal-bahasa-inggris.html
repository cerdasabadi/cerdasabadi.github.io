Contoh Personal Letter 1

Apt BLK 30, Eunos St. #05 – 116

Singapore 1441

2nd April 2009

Dear Liza,

I have received you letter asking me to meet you at your house this Sunday to discuss the urgent matter you have. I regret however to say that it will not be possible for me to see you on Sunday as I already have a previous appointment . I shall see you on Monday next week at 5 p.m . Daisy

Yang Tulus,

Daisy

Jawaban:

MARTABAK MANIS

Recipe Ingredients:

225 grams of all-purpose flour25 grams of cornstarch1 large egg1/2 tsp vanilla2 tbsp milk powder3 tbsp sugar300 ml water + (last 50 ml pour)A pinch of salt1/2 tsp double acting baking powder1/2 tsp baking soda2 drops of egg yolk coloringButter (Toppings)Sweetened condensed milk (Topping according to taste)Meses (Toppings according to taste)

Recipe Instructions:

Step 1, make the dough: Take a bowl, mix the flour, cornstarch, sugar, milk powder, a pinch of salt and baking powder, mix well then pour 300 ml of water then mix on medium speed for 10-15 minutes. Don't forget to trim the edges of the mixer every now and then.

Step 2, let stand then add the eggs and baking soda solution: Let the dough rest for 1-2 hours, then add the eggs, mix well and also add the baking soda solution with 50 ml of water, mix well. No need to shake it too long.

Step 3, heat the mold: Heat the mold on low heat until it is really hot. Divide the dough into 2 parts.

Step 4, cook the dough: Pour the batter into the preheated pan. Wait until the surface is foamy / there are small holes, sprinkle enough sugar to cover until cooked.

Step 5, remove the martabak: After it is cooked, remove the martabak and cover it briefly with a napkin or cover the pot and then add the topping to your liking, don't forget to brush with butter and the surface of the martabak to make it more moist. Cut and serve.

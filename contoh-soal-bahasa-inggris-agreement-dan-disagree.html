1. It was a miracle that neither the passengers nor the driver ____ seriously injured in the accident.
A. they were
B. was
C. to be
D. were
E. he was
Pembahasan :
Penggunaan dua kalimat digabungkan dengan Neither ... nor
Rumus yang digunakan : neither + noun + nor + singular noun + singular verb
sehingga, the driver ; singular noun == was (jawaban B)
Jawaban : B
2. The installation on various high-tech electrical devices in our office ___ a sophisticated safety system.
A. requiring
B. requires
C. to require
D. require
E. it requires
Pembahasan :
The installation == ( subyek tunggal)
maka, The installation on ... requires (verb tunggal)
Jawaban : B

33. 10 kalimat ekspresi agreement disagreementdalam bahasa inggris


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: agreement and disagreement
Pembahasan:

Expression of agreement:
- I agree with you.
- I couldn't agree more.
- I have the same view as him.
- I suppose I agree with her.
- I share the same opinion as her.

Expression of disagreement:
- I don't agree with you.
- I don't suppose I could agree with him.
- I don't share the same thought as him.
- I'm not in the same page as her.

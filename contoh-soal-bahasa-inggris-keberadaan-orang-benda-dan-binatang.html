3. contoh meminta dan memberi informasi terkait sifat orang, binatang,benda, sesuai dengan kontek penggunaannya dalam bahasa Inggris


Asking for and Providing Information

Asking for information adalah kegiatan menanyakan suatu informasi. Providing information adalah memberi jawaban atau informasi atas pertanyaan dalam asking for information.

Untuk meminta informasi terkait sifat orang, binatang, atau benda, kita dapat menggunakan kalimat-kalimat seperti "What is it like", "What does she look like?".

Untuk memberi informasi tentang orang, binatang, dan benda, biasanya digunakan kata sifat (adjectives). Kata sifat yang digunakan untuk mendeskripsikan orang, binatang, atau benda, misalnya: handsome, kind, smart, diligent, small, thick, cute, shady, crowded, adorable, messy, dan lain-lain.

Berikut beberapa contoh kalimat asking for information dan providing information.

[Asking for information] ? [Providing Information]

What do the elephants look like? They are big.What does Mrs. Lia look like? She is tall, with long hair and a pointed nose.What is Fizzy like? She is smart and friendly.What are tigers like? They are strong and fierce.Why do you like a cat? It has soft fur and cute face.Why do you like your new house? It is big, with complete facilities.What does your cat look like? It has white, black, and brown fur.What is your mother like? She is good at cooking.What does the new student look like? She is tall and thin.

Sama seperti bentuk kata sifat bila dalam bentuk kata tunggal, to be atau kata kerja (verb) yang menngikuti akan berubah seperti :

Rian is studying English (Rian sedang belajar Inggris)He lives in Jakarta now (dia tinggal di Jakarta sekarang)My uncle walks in the street (mereka berjalan di jalan)We are smart (kami pintar)Some lions are wild (beberapa singa adalah buas)All trees are dry (semua pohon gersang)

Bila dalam bentuk kata tunggal maka to be nya juga berubah seperti berikut :

He is smart (dia pintar)A lion is wild (singa adalah buas)The tree is dry (pohon itu gersang)
Orang : girl, boy, SintaTempat: park, supermarket, classroomHewan: cat, dog, snakeBenda: computer, laptop, bicycleKegiatan: concert, festival, Footbal Cupads

Kata Benda Tunggal

Kata benda tunggal (singular) adalah kata benda yang menunjukkan sebuah benda. Contoh kata benda tunggal adalah :

A window (sebuah jendela)One book (satu buku)An orange juice (sebuah jus jeruk)

Kata Benda Jamak

Kata benda jamak (plural) menunjukkan dua benda atau lebih. Contoh kata benda jamak adalah :

Two windows (dua jendela)Several books (beberapa buku)Three orange juice (tiga jus jeruk)

I like Elephants I like elephants. Elephants are the biggest land animals in the world. The African elephant is found on the continent of Africa and the Indian elephant is found in Asia. Elephants are mammals as well as herbivores, meaning they only eat plants rather than meat. I know that there are two main types of elephants; the African elephant and the Indian elephant. The African elephant is bigger than the Indian elephant. It has larger ears too. Both the males and females have tusks. The African elephant has wrinkly gray skin, a swayed back, and two tips at the end of its trunk that it can use like fingers to pick stuff up. The tusk make elephant look really cool. The Indian, or Asian, elephant is smaller than the African elephant and has smaller ears. They have more of a humped back and only one fingerlike tip at the end of their trunk. Also, their skin tends to be less wrinkly than the African elephant. Next time go to the zoo. I want to ride an elephant.

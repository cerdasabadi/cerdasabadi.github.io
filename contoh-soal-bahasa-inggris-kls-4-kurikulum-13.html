7. jawaban bahasa inggris kelas 9 kurikulum 2013 hal 13 dann 14


Jawaban buku Bahasa Inggris kelas 9 kurikulum 2013 halaman 13 dan 14 adalah tentang melengkapi dialog dengan complimenting and congratulating expression. Untuk melengkap dialog tersebut, kita harus memperhatikan situasi yang diberikan.

Pembahasan

Situation 2:

Lina has just finished drawing a picture of Mrs Ani , her english teacher. She will present it to her on her birthday. Siti praises the picture and she's sure that Mrs. Ani will like it.

Siti: “Lina, that is a beautiful picture. I am sure Mrs. Ani will like it.”

Beni:"Thanks. I hope so too."

Situation 3:

Udin has just finished making fried tofu for his friends. Dayu says that it looks nice and she’s sure that everybody will like it.

Dayu: “Great, your fried tofu looks nice. I am sure everybody will like it.”

Siti: “Thank you. I hope so too.”

Situation 4:

Beni has just finished mopping the floor of the health unit. Mr. Dani praises

him. He also thanks him for that.

Mr. Dani: “Good on you Beni. The floor of the health unit is clean now! Thanks.”

Beni: “You are welcome.”

Situation 5:

Siti has just finished practicing singing. She will sing in the celebration of the Kartini Day. Dayu praises her that she sings like a real singer, and she will get a big applause for that.

Siti: “You sing like a real singer. I’m sure you will get a very big applause.” 

Dayu: “Thank you. That’s very nice of you.”

Jadi, kalimat pertama pada situasi 2, 3 dan 5 adalah kalimat pujian (complimenting expression) yang kemudian diikuti dengan kalimat pengharapan (hoping expression) dengan menggunakan simple future tense (subject + will + verb 1).

Sedangkan pada situasi 4, kalimat pertama adalah complimenting expression yang kemudian diikuti dengan kalimat berterima kasih (thanking expression).

12. jawaban soal matematika hal 64 kurikulum 13 kls 8


Jawaban soal matematika hal 64 kurikulum 13 kls 8. Soal yang disajikan merupakan latihan soal “Ayo Kita Berlatih 2.3” tentang kedudukan garis terhadap sumbu x dan sumbu y yang terdiri dari 6 soal.

Garis x = k adalah garis yang sejajar sumbu y dan tegak lurus terhadap sumbu xGaris y = k adalah garis yang sejajar sumbu x dan tegak lurus terhadap sumbu y

dengan k adalah suatu konstanta

Pembahasan

Ayo Kita Berlatih 2.3

1. Gambarlah garis l yang tegak lurus pada sumbu x, berada di sebelah kanan dan berjarak 5 satuan dari sumbu y

Jawab

Karena l tegak lurus dengan sumbu x maka persamaan garisnya adalah x = kKarena berada disebelah kanan dan berjarak 5 satuan dari sumbu y maka k = 5

sehingga

persamaan garis l adalah  

x = k

⇒ x = 5

⇒ x – 5 = 0

(sejajar sumbu y)

2. Gambarlah garis m yang tegak lurus pada sumbu y, berada di bawah dan berjarak 4 satuan dari sumbu x

Jawab

Karena m tegak lurus dengan sumbu y maka persamaan garisnya adalah y = kKarena berada di bawah dan berjarak 4 satuan dari sumbu x maka k = –4  

sehingga

persamaan garis m adalah

y = k

⇒ y = –4  

⇒ y + 4 = 0

(sejajar sumbu x)

3. Jika ada garis a melalui titik B(4, 5) dan titik C(4, –5), bagaimanakah kedudukan garis tersebut terhadap sumbu x dan sumbu y

Jawab

Karena bilangan x nya sama yaitu x = 4, maka persamaan garis a adalah x = 4, sehingga kedudukan garis tersebut adalah

tegak lurus terhadap sumbu xsejajar dengan sumbu y dan berjarak 4 satuan terhadap sumbu y

4. Gambarlah garis k yang melalui titik P(–3, –5) yang tidak sejajar sumbu x dan sumbu y

Jawab

Agar garis k tidak sejajar sumbu x dan sumbu y, maka garis k harus sekaligus memotong sumbu x dan sumbu y serta melalui titik P(–3, –5)

Misal garis k tersebut memotong sumbu x di (2, 0) dan memotong sumbu y di (0, -2)

5. Apabila dua garis l dan m memotong sumbu x dan sumbu y tidak tegak lurus, bagaimanakah posisi garis l terhadap garis m? Jelaskan kemungkinannya dan tunjukkan dengan gambar

Jawab

Ada beberapa kemungkinan posisi garis l terhadap garis m yaitu

Garis l sejajar garis mGaris l berpotongan tegak lurus dengan garis mGaris l tidak berpotongan tidak tegak lurus dengan m

6. Diketahui garis l₁ melalui titik A(1, 0), garis l₂ melalui titik B(3, 0), garis l₃ melalui titik C(6, 0) dan garis l₄ melalui titik D(10, 0). Tentukan koordinat titik J pada garis l₁₀

Jawab

Kita lihat pola untuk l₁, l₂, l₃, l₄, ….

Pola x nya yaitu: 1, 3, 6, 10, ….Pola y nya yaitu: 0, 0, 0, 0, ….

Jadi  

nilai y untuk titik J pada garis l₁₀ adalah 0

nilai x untuk titik J pada garis l₁₀ adalah:

l₁: x = 1 = 1l₂: x = 3 = 1 + 2l₃: x = 6 = 1 + 2 + 3l₄: x = 10 = 1 + 2 + 3 + 4

sehingga

l₁₀: x = 1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + … + 10

a = 1 dan Un = 10

gunakan rumus Sn pada barisan aritmatika yaitu:  

Sn = [tex]\frac{n}{2}[/tex](a + Un)

⇒ x = S₁₀

⇒ x =  [tex]\frac{10}{2}[/tex] (1 + 10)

⇒ x = 5 (11)

⇒ x = 55

Jadi koordinat titik J pada garis l₁₀ adalah J(55, 0)

27. Jawaban buku oaket bahasa inggris kls 9 hal 24 kurikulum 2013


ituation 1:

Dayu, Udin and Lina are in front of the classroom, reminding each other that  they should do the chores before they go to school.

The conversation:

Lina: “My mom makes me do the housework before I go to school.”

Dayu: “I think you should. Your mom is right. You are a teenager now.”

Udin: “I agree with you Dayu. Our home is our home. If we live there, we  should take care of it ourselves.”

Situation 2:

Beni, Edo, and Udin are sitting in the library during the break. The furniture is  dusty. The books and the newspapers on the big table are messy.

The conversation:

Edo: “Don’t you think that our library looks dirty and messy? Let’s do  something to make it more tidy.”

Beni: “Why not? What if you dust the furniture, I tidy up the table, and Udin  sweep the !oor?”

Udin: “I agree with you. But let’s ask Ibu Tuti, our librarian, for permission  first.”

Situation 3:

Dayu, Siti, and Edo are sitting under a tree eating their lunch they have  brought from home.

Dayu: “I bring my lunch from home every day. I think everybody should  bring lunch to school. It saves a lot of money.”

Siti: “I agree with Lina. We should save money. We should not buy lunch in  the canteen. It is more expensive.”

Edo: “Yes, you are right. We can help our parents to save money.”

Situation 4:

Dayu’s mother was angry with her because she came home late yesterday.

Dayu: “My mom was a bit angry with me because I got home too late  yesterday, at three p.m. I went to the book shop, but I did not tell her.”

Beni: “I think she should. You should go home straight away after school.  You should not make your mom worried.”

Lina: “I agree. You should always tell your mom that you will go home late  if you have something else to do after school.”

Situation 5:

Siti, Udin, Lina are planning to walk to the hill next Sunday morning.

Siti: “What if we walk to the hill for exercise next Sunday morning. We  should have enough exercise to be healthy.”

Udin: “That’s a good idea. I will come. We should walk because walking is  healthy.”

Lina: “Let’s start from the front of the school at !ve thirty. The air is still very  fresh and cool. Don’t forget to bring fresh water, ok?”

Situation 6:

Edo, Dayu, and Lina are planning to visit their teacher, Mr. Sidin, who has  been in hospital for two days.

Siti: “Mr Sidin is sick and has been in hispital for two days. Why don’t we go  and see him this afternoon.”

Udin: “Yes, why not? If a teacher is sick, we should visit him to show our care  for him. Should we bring something for him?”

Lina: “Yes, we should bring something that will help him or the family go  through the time easily. Let’s chip in to buy bananas and oranges for  him and his family.”

Daily Routines

Daily routines atau Daily activities adalah kegiatan yang dilakukan sehari-hari. Kegiatan daily routines dalam Bahasa Indonesia sering juga disebut sebagai kebiasaan/ habit.

Saat kita ingin menyatakan kegiatan sehari-hari/ daily routines, kita perlu menggunakan Simple Present Tense. Simple Present Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang berlangsung sehari-hari (Daily routines).

Kata keterangan yang sering digunakan dalam simple present tense adalah:

• Every day

• Every week

• every month

• every year

• once a week

• twice a day

• three times a month

• three times a year

• always

• often

• usually

• seldom

• rarely

• dan lain lain

Pattern yang digunakan dalam simple present tense adalah:

(+) S + V1 atau S + V1s/es

(-) S + do/ does + V1

(?) Do/ DOes + S + V1?

NB:

V1 dan Do untuk subject I, You, They, We

V1s/es dan Does untuk subject He, She, It

Pembahasan

Daily Activities pada teks di atas:

The writer usually wakes up at 5.00 in the morning.He always brushes his teeth, and shaves, and washes his face after alarm wakes up him.He always gets dressed and goes downstair to have breakfast.He always has coffee, cereal and lots of fruits.He always goes work by bus and arrives at his office at 8.00.He finishes his work at 5.00 in the evening and goes home.He and his family always eat dinner in the dining room at around 6.00.They really enjoy watching their favourite TV programs together two or three times a week.He does exercise in the evening.At 9.30, he always goes to bed, reads for a while, and then falls a sleep.

ruas kiri

= sec⁴ A  - tan⁴ A

= (sec² A + tan² A) (sec² A -  tan² A)

= (sec² A + tan² A) (1/cos² A -  sin²A/cos²A)

= (sec² A + tan² A) { (1-  sin²A)/ (cos²A) }

= (sec² A + tan² A)  (cos²A/cos²A)

= (sec² A + tan² A) (1)

= sec² A +  tan² A

31. tan A / (secan A -1) + tan A / (secan A + 1) =​


Jawab:

2 . cosec A

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

(tan A / (secan A - 1)) + (tan A/(secan A + 1))

= (tan A . (secan A + 1) + tan A . (secan A - 1)) / (secan² A - 1)

= tan A . (secan A + 1 + secan A - 1) / tan² A

= 2 secan A / tan A

= 2/cos A . cos A/sin A

= 2/sin A

= 2 . cosec A

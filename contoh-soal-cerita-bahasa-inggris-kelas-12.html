10. buatkan contoh cerita bahasa inggris yg mengandung 12 tenses


Buatkan contoh cerita bahasa Inggris yang mengandung 12 tensesJawabanPendahuluan

Dalam bahasa Inggris terdapat 16 tenses yang merupakan pola penulisan kalimat yang masing-masing memiliki makna dan fungsi tersendiri.

Pembahasan

Berikut adalah contoh cerita bahasa inggris satu tema yang mengandung 12 tenses saling berkaitan:

Mickey is only 12-year-old (simple present tense), but he has finished high school (present perfect tense) and is currently studying robotic engineering in MIT (present continuous tense). He has been working hard on his project (present perfect continuous tense) to create a mind-controlled exoskeleton that enables the user to move around by 'ordering' the exoskeleton to do so. He believes his invention will be very helpful to mankind (present future tense), especially to disabled people who have lost their body motor control. He is very determined to develop this machine and hopes that those poor people will be using the machine in the near future (present future continuous) and thus be able to have a better life. He has set a strict project plan and aims to finish it within 2 months time. He projects that the control center helmet will have been ready for use by the end of this month (present future perfect tense), and hopefully people will have been using the machine by New Year (present future perfect continuous tense).

His quick progress is not of unfounded reason. When he was 5 years old, he lost his use of legs (simple past tense) after he had been severely injured in a motor accident (past perfect tense). He was accompanying his mother (past continuous) in the backseat of her car when a truck lost control and hit the car from the rear. Her mother regretted the accident very much, saying he would have been safe and sound at home (past future perfect tense) if she hadn't decided to bring him along.  Being a single parent, she had been raising Mike alone. (past perfect continuous tense) It was certainly a difficult time for the mother and son.

The owner of Prime Trading Hair and Wigs told detectives in Miami Gardens that he was notified overnight Tuesday that the alarm at the warehouse had been triggered.

Owner Rakib Hossain tells Miami television station WFOR that some of the products are worth up to $800 each.

Hossain says the thieves appeared to know what they were doing and were in the warehouse for no more than 5 minutes.

Video surveillance shows a truck backing up and ramming the warehouses front door three times.

Hossain says he is insured for his losses.

Writing Recount Text

I want to tell you a story about my failed holiday. So, a month ago me and my sister planned a holiday to Bali. We had our flight in the early morning around 6 a.m., but the night before we slept too late and didn’t get enough time to prepare ourselves.

Long story short, we made it to the taxi but unfortunately in the high way there was an accident which caused traffic. It was too long. We were afraid that we couldn’t catch our flight, even though there was enough time.

Finally we arrived fifteen minutes before boarding. My sister said “we are too late”, but I was insisting. And yes, she was right. The plane has departed. We were disappointed, and went back to our home.

Pembahasan

Recount text adalah sebuah bacaan yang memiliki fungsi untuk menceritakan kembali kejadian atau pengalaman di masa lalu. Tujuannya untuk menghibur dan memberikan informasi bagi para pembaca.

Ada beberapa macam recount text, pada teks soal merupakan contoh personal recount yang menceritakan ulang pengalaman pribadi penulis.

Struktur recount text:

Orientation - menceritakan awal mula kejadian dan siapa saja tokoh yang berperan juga tempat dan waktu kejadian.Events - runtutan peristiwa yang terdapat dalam alur cerita/pengalaman, biasanya terdiri atas beberapa bagian.Re-orientation - menceritakan tentang kesimpulan dan rangkuman cerita.

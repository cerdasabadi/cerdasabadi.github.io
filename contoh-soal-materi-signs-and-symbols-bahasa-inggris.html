The signs above are the rules and regulation on the street. There are various ways of telling rules.
- For the positive strong rules, you use: must or have to
- For negative strong rules: mustn't, can't, aren't allowed to
- For moderate optional rules: can, are allowed to, don't have to
So, here's the answer for those pictures:
1. Be careful for the long and winding road ahead! (you must be careful for such road)
2. This is the area for disabled people. Please be cautious!( you have to prioritize the disabled people first)
3. This is the crowded areas for pedestrians. (you must be careful with pedestrians)
4. This is the toilet room/ lavatory. (you can use the bathroom here)
5. Taking pictures are prohibited. (you can't take any pictures here)

1. map title

the map title shows the contents of a map.

2. Scale map

The map scale shows the ratio between the distance on the map and the actual distance in the field.

3. Map Symbols

Map symbols are special marks on the map that represent mapped objects.

4. Point symbols

The point symbols on the map can take many forms.

5. Line symbols

Line symbols can be drawn in various shapes and sizes of thickness.

6. Symbols of color

color symbols are used on maps by certain rules.

7.The urea symbol

Objects drawn on a map are usually illustrations of objects in the field.

8.Inset

An inset is a small map that is on a map to show the location of the mapped area among other locations that are better.

9. Coordinate lines

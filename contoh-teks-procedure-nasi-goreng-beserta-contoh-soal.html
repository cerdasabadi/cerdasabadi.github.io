3. tolong buatkan procedure text how to make nasi goreng. seperti contoh di atas:]​


Penjelasan:

How to Make Nasi Goreng (Indonesian Fried Rice)

Ingredients:

- 2 cups of cooked and cold white rice

- 2 tablespoons of cooking oil

- 2 cloves of garlic, minced

- 2 shallots, thinly sliced

- 2 eggs, beaten

- 100 grams of chicken, shrimp, or tofu (optional)

- 2 tablespoons of sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)

- 1 tablespoon of regular soy sauce

- 1/2 teaspoon of salt

- 1/4 teaspoon of pepper

- Sliced cucumber, tomato, and lettuce for garnish (optional)

- Fried shallots for topping (optional)

Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Ingredients:**

- Ensure that the rice is cooked and cold. It's best to use leftover rice.

- If using chicken, shrimp, or tofu, cut them into small pieces.

2. **Heat the Oil:**

- Heat the cooking oil in a large frying pan or wok over medium-high heat.

3. **Sauté the Aromatics:**

- Add the minced garlic and sliced shallots to the hot oil.

- Sauté them until fragrant and the shallots become translucent.

4. **Cook the Protein (Optional):**

- If using chicken, shrimp, or tofu, add them to the pan and cook until they are fully cooked through.

5. **Scramble the Eggs:**

- Push the cooked ingredients to one side of the pan and pour the beaten eggs into the empty side.

- Scramble the eggs until they are fully cooked.

6. **Combine and Stir-Fry:**

- Mix the scrambled eggs with the rest of the ingredients in the pan.

7. **Add the Rice:**

- Add the cold rice to the pan. Break up any clumps and mix everything together.

8. **Season with Sauces:**

- Pour in the sweet soy sauce and regular soy sauce. Stir to evenly coat the rice and ingredients.

9. **Season with Salt and Pepper:**

- Sprinkle salt and pepper over the mixture and continue to stir-fry until the rice is heated through and well combined with the sauces.

10. **Garnish and Serve:**

- If desired, garnish with sliced cucumber, tomato, lettuce, and fried shallots.

- Serve the Nasi Goreng hot and enjoy!

Now you have a delicious plate of homemade Nasi Goreng. Selamat makan! (Enjoy your meal!)

Ingredients

4 red potatoes1 tablespoon olive oil1 onion, chopped1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped2 tablespoons olive oil1 teaspoon salt3/4 teaspoon paprika1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper1/4 cup chopped fresh parsleyEquipment

Large skilletPlatePot

Steps
1.  Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes.
2.  Drain the potatoes and cut them into 1/2 inch cubes when they are already cool.
3.  In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon olive oil over medium high heat. Add onion and green pepper. Cook about 5 minutes stirring often, until soft. Transfer to a plate and set aside.
4.  Pour remaining 2 tablespoons of oil into the skillet and turn heat to medium-high.
5.  Add potato cubes, salt, paprika and black pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are  browned about 10 minutes.
6.  Stir in the onions, green peppers and parsley and cook for another minute. Serve hot.

PREP TIME: 20 Min
COOK TIME: 25 Min

Contoh teks prosedur membuat nasi goreng

Bahan-bahan  :
- 500 gram nasi putih
- 1 Butir Telur

- 6 sendok makan minyak untuk menumis
- 1 - 2 sendok makan saos tomat
- 3 siung bawang putih
- 6 siung bawang merah
- 3 - 5 buah cabai merah atau sesuai selera

- Garam secukupnya


Cara Membuat :
1.) Haluskan semua bumbu nasi goreng terlebih dahulu, setelah itu masukkan minyak ke dalam wajan, kemudian masukkan 1 butir telur lalu orak-arik hingga matang. Angkat dan dinginkan.
2.) Tumis bumbu yang telah dihaluskan hingga berbau harum.
3.) Masukkan nasi putih, lalu aduk-aduk hingga rata.
4.) Setelah rata, masukkan saos tomat, kecap manis dan telur, kemudian aduk hingga rata.
5.) Angkat dan nasi goreng siap untuk dihidangkan.

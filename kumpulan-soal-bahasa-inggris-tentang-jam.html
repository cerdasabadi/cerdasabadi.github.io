During holidays, my routine does not really differ that much, it starts at 4:30 AM by taking a shower, then I proceed to the TV room and watch until 5:30 AM. After that, I have breakfast at 6:00 AM, then I exercise until 7:00 AM. I take a rest at 1:00 PM, sleep until 4:00 PM, take another shower, have dinner at 8:00 PM, and then go back to sleep.

the verbs in the past participle form:

started

took

proceeded

watched

had

exercised

took

slept

took

had

went.

Penjelasan:

In the above passage, the verbs in the present participle form are changed into past participle form, which gives the sense of actions that has been completed in the past. And the past participle is formed by adding -ed, -d, -t, -en or -n to the base form of the verb, this is called regular verb.

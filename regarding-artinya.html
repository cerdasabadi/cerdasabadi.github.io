16. Mention the information regarding the personal achievement of the figure you chose


Achievement of Public Figure (Meryl Streep)Jawaban
Pendahuluan

Mary Streep (born June 22, 1949), is an American actress. Often described as the "best actress of her generation", Streep is particularly known for her versatility and accent adaptation. Nominated for a record 21 Academy Awards, she has won three.


Pembahasan

Here are some of Meryl Streep's achievements in her Hollywood career:

1. Streep has been nominated seventeen times at the Academy Awards, the most recorded by any actor, out of which she has won three times, once in the category of Best Supporting Actress and twice as Best Actress for the films, ‘Kramer vs. Kramer’, ‘Sophie's Choice’ and ‘The Iron Lady’.

2. Streep has been nominated fourteen times at the BAFTA Awards in various categories. She won the same twice in the category, Best Actress in a Leading Role for the films, ‘The French Lieutenant's Woman’ and ‘The Iron Lady’.

3. Streep gained a whopping 27 nominations at the Golden Globe awards, the highest recorded by any actor, in various categories. She eventually won the same eight times for the films, ‘Kramer vs. Kramer’, ‘The French Lieutenant's Woman’, ‘Sophie's Choice’, ‘Adaptation’, ‘Angels in America’, ‘The Devil Wears Prada’, ‘Julie & Julia’ and ‘The Iron Lady’.

4. Streep was thrice nominated at the Primetime Emmy Awards, eventually winning twice for ‘Holocaust’ and ‘Angels in America’.

5. Out of her ten nominations at the Satellite Awards, Streep won thrice for the works ‘Angels in America’, ‘The Devil Wears Prada’, and ‘Julie & Julia’.

6. The Screen Actors Guild Awards had twelve nominations of Streep’s films out of which she won twice for ‘Angels in America’ and ‘The Devil Wears Prada’.

7. Streep was inducted in the New Jersey Hall of Fame in 2008. This was not however the first time as she was earlier inducted in the Hollywood Hall of Fame in 1998.

8. Year 2010 witnessed Streep being elected to the American Academy of Arts and Letters. She was even presented with the National Medal of Arts by President Barack Obama the same year.

23. What is one notable achievement that Singapore is known for regarding its development?​


Jawaban:

One notable achievement that Singapore is known for regarding its development is its remarkable economic transformation and rapid growth. Singapore has transitioned from a developing nation with limited resources in the 1960s to one of the world's most prosperous and developed countries. Some key factors and achievements include:

1. **Economic Prosperity:** Singapore has established itself as a global financial and business hub. Its economy is highly developed, diversified, and competitive. It is known for its strong financial sector, trade, and investment-friendly policies.

2. **Education:** Singapore consistently ranks high in international education assessments. Its education system is renowned for producing highly skilled and competent students in various fields.

3. **Infrastructure:** Singapore boasts world-class infrastructure, including a modern transportation system, cutting-edge technology, and sustainable urban planning.

4. **Global Trade Hub:** The country's strategic location has made it a major center for international trade. The Port of Singapore is one of the busiest and most efficient ports globally.

5. **Innovation:** Singapore has invested heavily in research and development, fostering innovation and attracting talent from around the world. It is home to several leading research institutions and startups.

6. **Healthcare:** Singapore's healthcare system is highly regarded for its quality and accessibility. It consistently ranks among the top in global healthcare indexes.

7. **Political Stability:** Singapore has enjoyed a stable political environment since gaining independence in 1965. The government's efficient governance has played a crucial role in the nation's development.

8. **Quality of Life:** Singapore offers a high quality of life, with a strong focus on safety, cleanliness, and environmental sustainability.

9. **Smart Nation Initiative:** Singapore is actively pursuing its "Smart Nation" initiative, harnessing technology and data to enhance various aspects of life, from transportation to healthcare.

Penjelasan:

Overall, Singapore's transformation into a global economic powerhouse with a high standard of living is a testament to its effective governance, strategic planning, and commitment to development.

28. Fill in the table regarding conditional sentence type 2 and 3. ​


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

CONDITIONAL SENTENCES TYPE 2 & 3

Jawaban:

Fill in the table regarding conditional sentence type 2 and 3!

CONDITIONAL TYPE 2

Positive sentences

If she invited to her birthday party, I would come.If I were you, I would call him.If I had a lot of money, I would buy a mansion.If I were a bird, I would fly across the ocean.

Negative sentences

If I were you, I wouldn't buy that jacket.If it was rain, I couldn't come.If you did your homework, you wouldn't get punished.If your friend didn't arrive by four, you would leave without him.

CONDITIONAL TYPE 3

Positive sentences

If I had won the competition, I would have been happy.If he had saved enough money, he would have bought a new car.If we had known about this, we would have come yesterday.If I had met you yesterday, I would have invited you to the meeting.

Negative sentences

If I had come earlier, I would not have missed the train.If she had driven carefully, she wouldn't have had an accident.If it hadn't rained last weekend, we would have gone to the festival.If she had drunk coffee last night she wouldn't have slept well.

Penjelasan:

CONDITIONAL SENTENCES TYPE 2

Conditional tipe ini digunakan untuk menjelaskan situas yang tidak mungkin terjadi pada masa kini ataupun masa yang akan datang. Tipe ini sering disebut juga unreal condition.

Bentuk dari conditional type 2 adalah

If Clause                                              Main Clause

Past Simple                                         could/would/might+infinitive

CONDITIONAL SENTENCES TYPE 3

Conditional tipe ini adalah kalimat pengandaian untuk menjelaskan keadaan yang tidak mungkin terjadi pada masa lampau dan membayangkan hasil dari keadaan tersebut pada masa kini. Fakta dari kalimat berlawanan dengan kalimat tersebut.

Bentuk dari conditional type 3 adalah:

If Clause (condition)                              Main Clause (result)

If+ Past Perfect                                      S+had+Verb 3

If+ Past Perfect                                      S+would have+Verb 3

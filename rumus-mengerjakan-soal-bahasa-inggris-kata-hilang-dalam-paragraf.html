8. Paragraf 100 kata dalam bahasa inggris


Writing Recount Text

Last week, I went to the theatre, It was the only theatre in my town. I had one free ticket, but I didn’t know what I wanted to watch. Therefore, I decided to go to the theatre and see if there was any good movie I could watch.

I parked my motorcycle at the parking area and walked to the hall of the theatre. I took the free ticket from my wallet and asked the information how to use it. Unfortunately I missed the due date. Turns out the ticket was already expired two days ago. I went home with a great feeling of disappointment.

Pembahasan

Recount text adalah sebuah bacaan yang memiliki fungsi untuk menceritakan kembali kejadian atau pengalaman di masa lalu. Tujuannya untuk menghibur dan memberikan informasi bagi para pembaca.

Ada beberapa macam recount text, pada teks soal merupakan contoh personal recount yang menceritakan ulang pengalaman pribadi penulis.

Ciri-ciri recount text:

1. Menggunakan kalimat past tense - pada teks bacaan kita menemukan banyak sekali kalimat dengan bentuk lampau, salah satunya “We went there on night bus when we arrived in Cianjur, the bus stopped for a rest.”

2. Menggunakan action verbs - contoh pada bacaan “I got off the bus to get a cup of coffee…..”

3. Menggunakan adverb dan adverbial phrase untuk menjelaskan keterangan waktu dan tempat - contoh pada bacaan “One day…” “It took only a few minutes….”

4. Menggunakan conjunction dan time connectives untuk mengurutkan kejadian atau peristiwa - contoh pada bacaan “After several minutes…”

Reading Expository Text: Instruction/Procedure Text

Expository text adalah text yang menjelaskan, menginformasikan dan meng-edukasi pembacanya.

Beberapa contoh dari expository text adalah artikel berita, instruksi manual, text books, ensiklopedia dan journal.

PembahasanThe purpose of the text above is to inform us about how to use a new phone for the first time before making a phone call.What should we do after inserting the SIM card? We should insert the battery inside."These are the ways..." The synonym of the underlined word is method or technique.What does the text tell us about? The text tells us about the step-by-step in installing a new printer on our Windows laptop.What is the first operation we should take in installing our office printer on a windows laptop? The first operation is to get an IP address.The phrase of "a couple of copies" What does the sentence mean? It means that there are a few of CDs with the driver for the printer in the office. A couple in here doesn't always mean two, but it can be a few or several, mean more than two.What is the procedure about? The procedure about doing online check-in.What should we do before we enter the boarding gate? We should complete the check-in process, print the online boarding pass for each passenger and bring this with you."....you will be issued an online boarding pass..." The underlined word can be replaced by supply or provide.Make a procedure text: Topic How to buy an online-purchaseFirst, you have to decide what product you want to buy, for example you want to buy a new laptop.Go to the search engine and google for "new laptop" or if you already know the shop website you can directly go to that website.

Questions and Answers about Asking and Giving AttentionJawaban
Pendahuluan

Expressing attention adalah suatu ungkapan yang digunakan untuk meminta perhatian atau mengajak seseorang untuk fokus perhatiannya pada kita atau orang yang sedang berbicara.

Dalam mengungkapkan attention ada dua macam hal, yakni memberikan (giving) atau meminta (asking).


Contoh asking attention:

- Look!

- Listen!

- Hey!

- Hello?

- Attention, please?

- Excuse me?

- Sorry to bother you


Contoh giving attention:

- Tell me more about it.

- Really?

- What’s next?

- And then what?

- Is that all?

- I see.


Pembahasan

Andien: You know what, my new neighbor is very noisy! I could hear his voice all the way to my room. So loud.

Lala: Really?

Andien: Yeah, I don’t know what got him so loud. As if like nobody will hear him if he lower his volume.

Lala: I see.

Andien: So annoying.

Pembahasan

25. Andri: “Some of our friends will join the concert tomorrow. Would you like to go with me? What is your opinion about this?”

Sinta: “……… It will be very interesting. I need to refresh my mind this time.”

The suitable expression to complete the dialogue is………

Jawaban: a. What a great idea!

Penjelasan: Pada percakapan lanjutan, Sinta nampak setuju dan tertarik dengan ide pergi menonton konser bersama Andri dan teman-teman lainnya, sehingga ungkapan yang tepat untuk melengkapi adalah ‘what a great idea!’


26. Choose the irregular plural of the following word!

Jawaban: b. goose

Penjelasan: Irregular plural adalah sebuah kata benda yang artinya tidak menjadi jamak jika ditambahi akhiran -s atau -es. Sebagai contoh kata teacher, bentuk jamaknya adalah teachers, berarti kata tersebut bukan irregular plural. Dari semua pilihan jawaban yang tepat adalah goose, karena bentuk goose jamak menjadi geese.


27. Choose the regular plural of the following word!

Jawaban: c. boy

Penjelasan: Regular plural adalah kata benda yang jika ditambahi akhiran -s atau -es menjadi bentuk jamak. Pada pilihan jawaban man, tooth ox dan woman merupakan irregular plural, maka jawaban yang tepat adalah boy => boys.


28. “We use singular verb forms with school subjects.”

Choose the incorrect sentence according to this rule!

Jawaban: a. Maths isn’t my favorite subject

Penjelasan: Kalimat yang tidak tepat berdasarkan pernyataan di atas adalah A karena maths seharusnya ditulis dalam bentuk mathematics. Sementara itu nama mata pelajaran lainnya sudah benar dalam bentuk singular (tunggal)

Snow White

Once upon a time there lived a little girl named Snow White. She lived with her aunt and uncle because her parents were dead.

One day she heard her uncle and aunt talking about leaving Snow White in the castle because they both wanted to go to America and they didn’t have enough money to take Snow White.

Snow White didn’t want her uncle and aunt to do that so she decided that it would be best if she ran away. The next morning she ran away into the woods. She was very tired and hungry. Then she saw a little cottage. She knocked but no one answered so she went inside and fell asleep.

Meanwhile, the seven dwarfs were coming home from work. There they found Snow White sleeping. Then Snow White woke up. She saw the dwarfs.

The dwarfs said, “What is your name?”

Snow White said, “My name is Snow White.”

Then, Snow White told the dwarfs the whole story.

The dwarfs said, “If you want, you may live here with us.”

Finally, Snow White and the seven dwarfs lived happily ever after.

29. Soal pilihan ganda bahasa inggris tentang congratulation


Here are five multiple choice questions about congratulations in English:

1. Which of the following is the correct way to congratulate someone on their wedding?

a) "Congratulations on your divorce!"

b) "Congratulations on your engagement!"

c) "Congratulations on your wedding!"

d) "Congratulations on your new job!"

Answer: c) "Congratulations on your wedding!"

2. Which of the following is the correct way to congratulate someone on their birthday?

a) "Happy condolences on your birthday!"

b) "Happy condolences on your new job!"

c) "Happy birthday to you!"

d) "Congratulations on your birthday!"

Answer: c) "Happy birthday to you!"

3. Which of the following is the correct way to congratulate someone on their promotion?

a) "Congratulations on your demotion!"

b) "Congratulations on your new job!"

c) "Congratulations on your promotion!"

d) "Congratulations on your retirement!"

Answer: c) "Congratulations on your promotion!"

4. Which of the following is the correct way to congratulate someone on their new house?

a) "Congratulations on your new car!"

b) "Congratulations on your new house!"

c) "Congratulations on your new job!"

d) "Congratulations on your retirement!"

Answer: b) "Congratulations on your new house!"

5. Which of the following is the correct way to congratulate someone on their graduation?

a) "Congratulations on your graduation!"

b) "Congratulations on your new job!"

c) "Congratulations on your promotion!"

d) "Congratulations on your retirement!"

Answer: a) "Congratulations on your graduation!"

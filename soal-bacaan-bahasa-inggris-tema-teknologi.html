In a sunny afternoon, a group of students gathered in the university courtyard, excitedly posing for a group photo. They had just completed a successful project together and wanted to capture the moment. With smiles on their faces and laughter filling the air, they arranged themselves in a way that showcased their unity and friendship. The camera clicked, freezing the joyful atmosphere into a timeless memory. They knew that they would cherish this photo for years to come, a reminder of their shared journey and the bonds they had formed as college students.

Artinya: Pada sebuah siang yang cerah, sekelompok mahasiswa berkumpul di halaman universitas, dengan antusias berpose untuk foto bersama. Mereka baru saja menyelesaikan proyek bersama yang sukses dan ingin mengabadikan momen tersebut. Dengan senyum di wajah mereka dan tawa yang memenuhi udara, mereka menyusun diri dengan cara yang menunjukkan persatuan dan persahabatan mereka. Kamera berkedip, membekukan suasana kegembiraan menjadi kenangan abadi. Mereka tahu bahwa mereka akan menghargai foto ini selama bertahun-tahun, sebagai pengingat perjalanan bersama mereka dan ikatan yang terbentuk sebagai mahasiswa.

Contoh paragraf bahasa Inggris dengan tema "aku sedang membaca buku di perpustakaan":

On a quiet afternoon, I found myself engrossed in a book at the library. Surrounded by rows of shelves filled with knowledge, I felt a sense of tranquility and curiosity. As I flipped through the pages, the words came alive, painting vivid pictures in my mind. The world around me faded away as I delved deeper into the story, immersed in the author's words. Time seemed to stand still, and I was transported to different places and eras through the power of imagination. The library became my sanctuary, a place where I could escape into the realms of literature and expand my horizons.

Artinya: Pada sebuah siang yang tenang, saya menemukan diri saya asyik dalam membaca buku di perpustakaan. Dikelilingi oleh rak-rak berisi pengetahuan, saya merasakan ketenangan dan rasa ingin tahu. Saat saya membalik halaman demi halaman, kata-kata itu menjadi hidup, melukis gambaran yang jelas di dalam pikiran saya. Dunia di sekitar saya memudar seiring saya semakin terbenam dalam cerita, tenggelam dalam kata-kata sang penulis. Waktu terasa berhenti, dan saya dibawa ke tempat-tempat dan era yang berbeda melalui kekuatan imajinasi. Perpustakaan menjadi tempat perlindungan saya, tempat di mana saya bisa melarikan diri ke dunia sastra dan memperluas wawasan.

New inventions in the field of science and technology are very necessary in such a modern world for a country to be strong and well developed country than other countries. In this competitive world, we need more technology to go ahead and become a successful person in the life.

Development, whether it is human development or country development, is linked to the proper growth and development of the technology in many ways. Technological advancement happens when there become new inventions in the science by highly skilled and professional scientists. We can say that technology, science and development are equally proportional to each other. Development in the science and technology is very necessary for the people of any nation to go hand in hand together by the people of other countries. Development of the science and technology depends on the analysis and proper understanding of facts. Development of technology depends on the way of application of various scientific knowledge in right direction.

What improvement we are seeing in our life on daily basis is because of the science and technologies. For the proper growth and development of the country, it is very necessary to go science and technology hand in hand. Villages are getting developed to towns and towns to cities thus expanding the greater horizons of economy. Our country India is a fast developing country in the sense of science and technology.

The implication of science and technology to the people’s life is very old from the time of Indus Valley Civilization. It was almost first invention when came to know about fire and wheel. Both of the inventions are considered as the mother of all the technological innovations of the modern time. Through the invention of fire people knew about the power of energy first time. Since then, people’s curiosity was increased and they started trying their hard to research about various measures to make life style easy and simple.

Some examples of the advancement in the technologies are establishment of railway system, metro system, railway reservation system, internet, super computers, mobiles, smart phones, online access of people in almost every area, etc. Government of India is creating more opportunity to the space organization and several academic institutions (Indian Association for the Advancement of Science) for the better technological growth and development in the country. N. Bose, C. V. Raman, Dr. Homi J. Bhabha, Srinivasa Ramanujan, father of India’s nuclear power, Dr. Har Govind Singh Khorana, Vikram Sarabhai, etc.

Science and technology plays vital role in the modern life and profoundly influenced the course of human civilization. Technological advancement in the modern life has provided us lots of remarkable insights all over the world. Scientific revolutions has taken its full speed from the 20th century and has become more advance in the 21st century. We have entered to the new century in new ways and with all the arrangements for well being of the people. Modern culture and civilization has become dependent over the science and technologies as they have become integral part of life according to the need and requirement of the people.

Technological development in any filed enhances the economy of any nation. In order to improve the power of science and technology in India, Indian government has made Council of Scientific and Industrial Research in the year 1942 and Board of Scientific and Industrial Research in the year 1940. In order to emphasize the growth of science and technology in the country, Indian government has established a chain of national laboratories and research institutes in various regions.

30. Jawab soalnya berdasarkan bacaan diatasnya ( jawaban menggunakan bahasa inggris )​


Answer:The topic of the text is: a skyway cable carWriter's intention is: to describe a skyway cable carInformation to get: a skyway cable car has bright colors, hung on solid cable wire and travels around 3.38 kmThe main idea of first paragraph: the writer's question about a skyway cable carThe main idea of second paragraph: the skyway cable car looks likeThe main idea of third paragraph: the way to go to the peak by the skyway cable carPresent tense is the most used tensePresent tense is used for description text to describe the fact of the object in a present way

—

Discussion:

Descriptive text merupakan teks yang membahas suatu penggambaran obyek yang dituju oleh penulis untuk diperoleh informasi tersebut bagi pembaca sehingga mengenal obyek yang dideskripsikan lebih dekat.

Dalam descriptive text berfungsi untuk:

to describe people, places, or things in casual and actual (untuk mendeskripsikan orang-orang, tempat-tempat, atau benda-benda secara santai dan mengacu kenyataan)

Descriptive text memiliki ciri-ciri seperti:

dituliskan dalam present tense (cth: the cable car has bright colors)tidak merubah fakta dari obyek yang dideskripsikan (cth: fish can stay alive out of water for a year)terdapat gaya penulisan yang kasual (cth: have you ever seen how's skunk sleeping?)

Explanation:

Halo! Untuk mendapatkan jawaban yang diberikan, amati terlebih dahulu teksnya sehingga menemukan jawaban yang persis.

Berikut adalah pembuktian untuk jawaban yang saya berikan:

nomor 1

Teks yang diberikan adalah descriptive text karena mendeskripsikan trem yang pernah dinaiki oleh penulis. Hal tersebut yang disebut sebagai topik atau pembahasan teks tersebut.

—> jawabannya: a skyway cable car

nomor 2

Penulis menuliskan teks deskripsi tersebut dengan memerhatikan social function of descriptive text yaitu:

to describe people, places, or things in casual and actual

Sehingga dapat disimpulkan bahwa penulis mendeskripsikan skyway cable car merupakan tujuannya adanya teks deskripsi yang dicatat olehnya.

—> jawabannya: to describe a skyway cable car

nomor 3

16. berapakah banyak susunan huruf berbeda dapat dibuat dari huruf pada kata-kata dibawah ini.a. BROKOLIb. REFORMASIc. TRAGEDI​


Jawaban:

a) BROKOLI

Jml huruf = 7

Jml huruf yg sama, O = 2

P = 7! / 2!

P = 7 . 6 . 5 . 4 . 3

P = 2520 susunan

b) REFORMASI

Jml huruf = 9

Jml huruf yg sama, R = 2

P = 9! / 2!

P = 9 . 8 . 7 . 6 . 5 . 4 . 3

P = 181440 susunan

c) TRAGEDI

Jml huruf = 7

Jm huruf yg sama, tdk ada = 1

P = 7! / 1!

P = 7 . 6 . 5 . 4 . 3 . 2

P = 5040 susunan

23. tuliskan kronologi tragedi mandor dalam bahasa inggris​


Jawaban:

MANDOR KILLING FIELDS

The site of large-scale massacres committed by Japan during WWII in this remote West Kalimantan location during their occupation of much of what today is Indonesia. This is commemorated through a big memorial with graphic depictions of Japanese brutality; there is also an underdeveloped exhibition pavilion, and along a forest drive you can find several mass graves of the victims.

 

It's quite an off-the-beaten-track and very special dark site that may perhaps hardly be worth the journey on its own, but it combines extremely well with some of Borneo's other prime attractions.

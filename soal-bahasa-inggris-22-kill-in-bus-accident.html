5. effect: afraid and got amnesia
sentence : after the car accident, i'm afraid and got amnesiaConnectives are used to show cause and effect.

Kata penghubung di gunakan untuk menunjukkan sebab akibat.

It is used to connect two sentences which has distinct ideas.

Ini digunakan  untuk menghubungkan 2 kalimat yang memiliki ide berbeda.

Connectives ( Therefore, because, because of, due to, caused of, as a result , as a consequence, owing to, since )

-----------------------------------------------------------------

Answer :

1. Cause  : it was very windy

Effect    : All the flights were cancelled

Sentence : it was very windy; therefore , all the flights were cancelled.

2. Cause : She ate too much

Effect    : She got stomachache

Sentence : She ate too much. As a consequence, she got stomachache.

3. Cause : I ran out of money

Effect    : i couldn't travel to Paris.

Sentence : I ran out of money, and as a result, i could  not travel to Paris.

4. Cause : He is afraid to fly

Effect : He gets panic attack when he rides the air balloon.

Sentence :

* Since he is afraid to fly, he gets panic attack  when he rides the air balloon.

* Because he is afraid to fly, he gets panic attack when he rides the air balloon.

5. Cause : After the car accident

Effect : He sustained a concussion

Sentence :

*Owing to  the car accident, he sustained a concussion.

* After the car accident,  he sustained a concussion.

--------------------------------------------------

Cause and Effect Connectives

14. What's the difference from Mr, Sir, Ms, Mrs, Miss, Madam, Ma'am??​


1. Mr: It is a shortened form of "Mister" and is used when addressing a man that you don't know very well.

2. Sir: It is generally used for a man of any age that is older or higher in authority.

3. Ms: It is used for a woman regardless of her status.

4. Mrs: It is used for a woman that is married.

5. Miss: It is used for a woman that is not yet married.

6. Madam: It is generally used for a woman that is older or higher in authority.

7. Ma'am: It is a shortened form of Madam.

18. 1. Note about Application Letter Outline Salutation If you begin with "Dear Sir" or "Dear Madam", end with "Yours faithfully". If you begin with a name, end with "Yours sincerely".​


Jawaban:

Yes, that is correct. When writing an application letter, the way you address the recipient in the salutation can affect the closing. If you begin with "Dear Sir" or "Dear Madam", it is appropriate to end with "Yours faithfully". If you begin with a name, it is appropriate to end with "Yours sincerely".

36. Bagaimana perbedaan dalam penggunaan paggilan mr. dan sir dalam bahasa Inggris?​


Jawaban:

Mr : panggilan untuk seorang laki laki yang sudah menikah/berkeluarga

Sir: sering digunakan untuk menyapa seseorang dalam penulisan surat, seperti “Dear Sir”. Contoh sederhana dari penggunaan “Mr.” dan “Sir” ialah ketika kita memanggil bapak guru di sekolah, kita biasa memanggilnya “Pak”, maka dalam bahasa Inggris menjadi “Sir”.

Jawaban:

Kata Sir mempunyai makna sama dengan Mr, tapi biasanya sesorang menggunakan sapaan sir kepada laki-laki dewasa yang tidak begitu dekat. Jadi, pemggunaan sapaan Sir ini digunakan sebagai panggilan tanpa nama.

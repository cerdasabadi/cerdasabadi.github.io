2. KK bantu jawab soal English Buat dialog dalam bahasa Inggris ​


Jawaban:

A: Hey, you look not well. Are you okay? (Hei, kamu terlihat tidak baik. Apakah kamu tidak apa-apa?) B: My stomach hurts. (Perutku sakit) A: Do you need anything, like a medicine maybe? (Apakah kamu butuh sesuatu, seperti obat mungkin?) B: Yes, please. I need a medicine for a stomachache. (Ya. Aku butuh obat untuk sakit perut) A: I will get that for you. (Aku akan mengambilkannya untukmu) B: Thank you. (Terima kasih) 5 minutes later... (5 menit lemudian...)

A: There is no medicine for upset stomach in our medicine storage box, but I bring this eucalyptus oil for you. Just rub it on your belly. It can ease the pain. (Tidak ada obat untuk menyembuhkan sakit perutmu di kotak obat kita, tetapi aku bawa minyak kayu putih untukmu. Oleskan di perutmu. Itu akan mengurangi rasa sakit) B: Thanks. (Terima kasih) A: If the pain persists, you should see a doctor. (Jika rasa sakitnya tidak hilang, kamu harus pergi ke dokter) B: Okay. (Oke)

A dialogue that contains expressions of:

A. Asking for adviceB. Giving adviceC. Responding to adviceD. Offeing something/helpE. Accepting/Refusing an offerJAWABAN ⬇️

Rara: Why do you look so sad?

Kenapa kamu terlihat sangat sedih?

Riri: I am sad because my grade is bad in English test.

Aku sedih karena nilai tes bahasa Inggris saya jelek.

Rara: What grade did you get in English test?

Berapa nilai yang kamu dapatkan dalam tes bahasa Inggris?

Riri: I got D. I really want to improve my grade in the next test. What should I do? (A)

Aku mendapat D. Aku mau meningkatkan nilai saya di tes selanjutnya. Apa yang harus aku lakukan?

Rara: Keep calm ri, don't be sad. You should study harder than before. (B)

Tenang Ri, jangan sedih. Kamu bisa belajar lebih giat dari sebelumnya.

Riri: Thank you for the advice. (C)

Makasih ya atas sarannya.

Rara: You are welcome. Btw, why don’t we study together? So, I can help you. (D)

Sama-sama. Ngomong-ngomong, bagaimana kalau kita belajar bersama? Jadi, aku bisa membantumu.

Riri: That's good idea! I’m in. (E)

Itu ide yang bagus! Aku setuju.

PEMBAHASAN 1. Suggestion / advice

➡️ Asking for advice adalah menanyakan saran

(a) What should I do? ✔️

➡️ Giving advice adalah memberikan saran

(b) You should study harder than before.✔️

➡️ Responding to advise adalah respon terhadap saran yang diberikan

(c) Thank you for the advice ✔️

➡️ Accepting an offer adalah Respon menerima terhadap tawaran

(e) That's good idea! ✔️

14. tuliskan pidato dalam bahasa inggris tentang english is important


The Importance of English

Good Afternoon,

Honorable all the juries and the committee of this English speech contest.

First of all, let’s thank to Allah SWT who has given us many mercies and blessing so we can join this program without any troubles. Secondly, I would like to introduce myself.

My name is ……. I am a student of …..

Well, ladies and gentlemen, in this occasion I would like to tell you about the importance of English for us.

Ladies and gentlemen,

English is an International language, so it’s very important for us to learn English. Some of us may be think that it’s not necessary to be able to master English because we live in Indonesia, we speak Indonesian language, and all our families are Indonesian

people. The others think that English is difficult lesson, so they are reluctant to learn

English. But from now, we have to change our mind. Why? Ladies and gentlemen,

All of us knew have known that English is an International language. There are so many advertisements in the newspapers look for people who has ability in English. It means that there are so many business companies want to look for the employees who have ability in English. We have graduated from the university. We have knowledge, but if we can’t compete with other people because we can’t speak and write English, it’ so poor for us. That’s one of the reasons why we have to learn English.

My friends,

Now we live in modern era, we have to compete with the people from other countries. We have to move on. It will happen if can communicate in English, right? There are so many information we can get from the internet. It’s very useful for us as students. By internet, we can find information about everything to support our school needs. It will be easier if we know English well.

Ladies and gentlemen,

The government has students exchange program. The program is held to exchange the

Indonesian students with other countries’ students. They will know the culture and many things about Indonesia, and so do we. If we want to join in this program, of course we have to be able to communicate in English. Don’t we want to join it? I, myself, really want to join it if there is a chance. I’m sure that you want too.

Ladies and gentlemen,

We have known the importance of English. From now, don’t be lazy to learn English! By trying and trying, I’m sure we can make it. Let’s do it now! Let’s do our best!

I think that all what I can tell you. Thank you for your attention. Assalamu’alaikum wr. Wb.

MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS
KELAS : VII
MATERI : TENSES
KATA KUNCI : SIMPLE PRESENT
KODE SOAL : 5
KATEGORISASI : 7.5

Jika kalimat nya bentuk simple present, kalimat di atas sudah benar. Dan untuk menerjemahkan bahasa inggris tidak perlu ditambahkan "language", karena kata "english" sendiri sudah bermakna "bahasa inggris".

Dan karena subject nya adalah "I" maka penggunaan kata kerjanya sudah benar yaitu "V1" tanpa tambahan s/es.

34. Jawab 2 soal aja Pelajaran:Math English(matematika dalam bahasa inggris)​


Questions

if x = 2 and y = 1 when value:

a. 2x - y³

b. -x² - 4y

Answer

a. 2x - y³ = 2(2) - (1)³

= 4 - 1

= 3

b. -x² - 4y = -(2)² - 4(1)

= -4 - 4

= -(4 + 4)

= -8

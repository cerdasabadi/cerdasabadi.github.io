PENDAHULUAN

The Purpose/Tujuan Invitation (Undangan). “To invite someone attend/come an event.” Untuk mengajak atau mengundang seseorang.

JAWABAN

[tex]Invitation \: and \: its \: Response[/tex]

READING

Task 1

Read the following text! Read the text clearly and fluently!

To: All my beloved friends and family

Please, Come to my 10th birthday party! And don't forget to bring your sincere present.

Place : KFC Jln. Pahlawan No. 10 Banda Aceh

Attire : Casual Attire

Time : 09.00 p.m - 12.00 p.m

Date : 26th December 2018

I'm waiting for your coming! Come before 10.00 pm and wear your casual attire!

My sincere greeting,

Annie

Task 2

State T for true statements and F for false statements based on the invitation above!

1. Statements : The invitation above is for Annie's friend.

T/F : T

2. Statements : The party will be held for three hours.

T/F : T

3. Statements : The occasion above is about the birthday.

T/F : T

4. Statements : Annie lives far from Banda Aceh.

T/F : T

5. Statements : The text above is included into the invitation text.

T/F : T

PEMBAHASAN

Giving Invitation:

1) Would you come to my house at 07.00

o'clock?

2) May you come to my birthday party?

3) Do you mind if you come to my office at

02.00 o'clock?

4) Would you come to Jatim Park with me after the class?

5) Would you visit me after dinner?

6) Could you come to school next Sunday?

7) Could you come to my house to have

lunch wit me?

8) May you come to the orphanage?

9) Do you mind if you come to the Zoo?

10) May you come to my party this night?

Respond:

-Accept

1. I'd love too.

2. That sound good.

3. All right I'll be there.

4. It's okay

5. Never mind.

-Refuse

1. I'm sorry, I can't.

2. I'd love too but I have something to do.

3. I'm really sorry but I have to finish my

homework.

4. I'm not sure, I can't.

5. Well, thanks, but I can't.

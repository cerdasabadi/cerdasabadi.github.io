9. Diana says, “That's neat and stylish!”What does she express?​


Complimenting expressions adalah ungkapan positif kepada seseorang karena telah melakukan sesuatu yang baik. Tujuannya adalah memberikan apresiasi atau motivasi positif kepada orang tersebut.

Diana says, “That's neat and stylish!”

Diana berkata, "Itu rapi dan keren!"

What does she express?​ She gives complimenting expression.

Pembahasan

There are two kinds of complimenting expressions you can use:

1. Compliment using a simple sentence

Examples:

I like your hat = Thank you (Saya suka topimu = Terima kasih)That is a nice shoes = Thanks for your compliment (Itu sepatu bagus = Terima kasih atas pujianmu)That's good = Oh thanks (Itu bagus = Oh terima kasih) You look great in that dress = I'm glad you like it (Kamu terlihat keren dengan gaun itu = Aku senang kamu menyukainya)

2. Compliment using "What a ..." or "How ..."

Examples:

What a beautiful scarf! = Thank you (Scarf yang benar-benar bagus = Terima kasih)How cool it is! = Thank you very much (Betapa kerennya ini = Terima kasih banyak)How handsome you are! = Oh thanks (Betapa tampannya kamu = Oh terima kasih)What a great house = Thank you for your compliment (Rumah yang benar-benar bagus)

26. Apa bedanya like dan likes bahasa inggris


Like, sebagai kata kerja, berarti suka/menyukai.

Penggunaan like atau likes tergantung pada subject-nya. Bila subject tunggal, menggunakan likes. Bila subject jamak, menggunakan like.

I, you, they, we = like

she, he, it, name = likes

contoh dalam kalimat

1. I like to eat fried chicken

2. She likes to drink orange juice.

3. We like to play football.

4. My father likes to read magazine

5. The students like to study in the library.

6. Everyone likes to sing that song together.

Everyone, meski artinya setiap orang tetapi tetap dianggap tunggal, karena everyone merupakan kata ganti (pronoun).

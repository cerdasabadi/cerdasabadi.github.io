fact: Indra missed the train so he didn't attended the meeting

fact: Tari drave her car fast so she didn't arrived late
4. If ardy had worked hard he wouldn't have been fired.
fact: Ardy didn't work hard so he was fired
5. If Rasti hadn't played tennis she wouldn't have broken her leg.
fact: Rasti played tennis so she broke her leg

5. kak bantu buatin 1 paragrph bahasa inggris di dalam nya ada kalimat conditional type 1


Sebelum membuat text dalam bahasa Inggris menggunakan conditional type 1, sebaiknya kita memahami dulu bentuk conditinal type 1.

Conditional Sentences type 1 adalah kalimat pengandaian. Kalimat pengandaian ini berdasarkan fakta yang ada dan digunakan untuk menyatakan situasi yang mungkin terjadi atau tidak mungkin terjadi.

Pada umumnya kalimat pengandaian (conditional sentences) terdiri dari dua bagian yaitu "main clause" dan "if clause." Jadi, pada kalimat conditional sentences type 1 ini,  kondisi pada "main clause" akan terjadi jika syarat -syarat pada "if clause" terpenuhi.

Secara penulisan tidak masalah apabila anda menuliskan "main clause" terlebih dahulu atau "if clause" terlebih dahulu.

Formula untuk Conditional Sentences Type 1 :

                     If + Subject + Verb (present), S + Will + Verb 1

                                              atau

                     Subject + Will + Verb 1, if +Subject

PEMBAHASAN

Contoh text menggunakan conditional type 1 :

Today, I have a lot of works to do. I try to manage to finish all of them. So, I think if I come home early I will be able to do my homework. I write down all the tasks that I have to do that day, if the task finish, I will mark them "done".

18. Apa contoh type 1 conditional dalam bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

Conditional Sentence type 1 digunakan ketika kita ingin mengungkapkan sesuatu yang memiliki kemungkin akan terjadi di masa yang akan datang.

Rumusnya

If+simple present, simple future (will)

Contoh:

"If i come home early, i will get some rest"

"Jika aku pulang lebih awal, aku akan beristirahat"

Nah jadi maksudnya jika kamu pulang lebih awal, kemungkinan untuk bisa beristirahat itu ada dan bisa terjadi.

CONTOH

If it rains, you will get wet.

You will get wet if it rains.

If Sally is late again I will be mad.

I will be mad if Sally is late again.

If you don't hurry, you will miss the bus.

You will miss the bus if you don't hurry.

37. contoh dialog bahasa inggris type 2 dan type 3


Jawaban:

Contoh Dialog Bahasa Inggris Type 2:

Person A: Hey, have you ever been to Bali?

Person B: Yes, I have. I went there last year for a vacation. It was amazing!

Person A: Really? What did you do there?

Person B: Well, I visited some beautiful beaches like Kuta and Nusa Dua. I also explored the cultural sites like Tanah Lot Temple and Ubud Monkey Forest.

Person A: That sounds incredible! I've always wanted to visit Bali. Did you try any traditional Balinese food?

Person B: Yes, I did. I tried their famous dish called Babi Guling and it was delicious!

Person A: I should definitely plan a trip to Bali soon. Thanks for sharing your experience!

Contoh Dialog Bahasa Inggris Type 3:

Person A: I wish I could play the guitar like you. You're so talented!

Person B: Thank you! It took a lot of practice and dedication to get to where I am now.

Person A: How long have you been playing?

Person B: I've been playing for about 10 years. I started when I was in high school and never stopped.

Person A: That's impressive. Do you have any tips for someone who wants to learn?

Person B: Sure! The key is to practice regularly and be patient with yourself. Start with the basics and gradually build up your skills. It's also helpful to take lessons or learn from online tutorials.

Person A: I'll keep that in mind. Hopefully, I can become as good as you someday.

Person B: I have no doubt that you can. Just keep practicing and never give up on your passion for music. Good luck!

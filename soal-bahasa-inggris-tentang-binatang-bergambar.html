After getting the food we went to the monkey cage, where we saw and feed some monkey. Then we went to the nocturnal zone where we saw some birds and reptiles which only come out at night after the sunset to found some food.

Before lunch we got a chance to ride on the elephant. It was very fun. I nearly fell off when trying to get on the elephant’s back. During lunch we enjoyed the sound of birds in the park.
